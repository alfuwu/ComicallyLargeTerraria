Are your muscles too big for Terraria's tiny little baby items? Do you have fever dreams of giant, sexy Draxes mowing down stone?
Then this mod will satisfy all of your worldly needs!

(By installing this mod, the client agrees that Comically Large Co. is not responsible for any death nor injury caused by wielding a Comically Large item)

This mod was created 'cause I saw someone with a kinda big pickaxe that one time. And I felt like I had to have a bigger pickaxe.

Content: Adds customizable (via config) modifier 'Comically Large' that can be applied to most items that can have a modifier, which increases an item's size by comical amounts.

All source code is available via extraction through tModLoader.
